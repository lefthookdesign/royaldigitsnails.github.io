Meet the Manicurist

Specializing in the maintenance and beautification of healthy, natural nails. Royal Digits is owned by state licensed manicurist Crystal Curry, and operates in Fort Jones, CA.

Offering top of the line services using the latest industry techniques and expert care for ladies and gentleman of all ages. View our services

Nail services are sometimes viewed as purely a cosmetic luxury, but properly caring for your hands and feet is important for your (physical and mental) health. As we age it becomes increasingly difficult to properly care for our hands and feet by ourselves, this is why Royal Digits caters to clients of advanced age or clients with mobility limitations.
